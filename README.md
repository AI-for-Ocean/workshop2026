# AI4Ocean Workshop 2026
Based on feedback that the three-day 2025 workshop at Caltech was too short, we have extended the 2026 workshop to two weeks. Additionally, we are co-hosting this year's event with the Data Science in Physical Oceanography program at the University of Washington.
# AI4Ocean Workshop 2026 — Draft Agenda

**Dates:** July 20–31, 2026
**Venue:** School of Oceanography, Ocean Science Building, University of Washington
**Status:** DRAFT — semifinal

> Times listed in Pacific Time.
> Invited talks: 20 min presentation + 20 min discussion (40 min total).
> Lightning talks: 10 min presentation + 10 min discussion (20 min total).
> Daily structure: 4 sessions (2 morning, 2 afternoon) with 30-min coffee breaks and 90-min lunch.
> Simple breakfast (muffins and bagels) and Lunch box provided for the first three days.
